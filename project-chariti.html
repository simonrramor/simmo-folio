    Simon Amor (Designer)
After moving to London, I wanted to do something good in my free time by participating in charity work and volunteering in community projects. It didn't take long for me to realise just how hard it was to find local charity events and sign up for them. I decided to design an event based app to solve the problem of finding charity and volunteer work in your area.
The sign up process is quick and simple, with the option of logging in via facebook, and allows people to start looking for events as soon as possible.
With permission, the app will locate the user's current position. From this the users can then select a search radius in which they would like to attend events.
An events screen shows all the upcoming events within the search radius. Users can filter by popularity, date, location or even whether friends are going to. Users can also see events coming up that they have registered for. If they are yet to register for an event they will be prompted to find one on the search screen.
Free time is rare, and people like to plan ahead. Users can select dates they wish to attend events in advance for a more targeted search.
A comment section allows people to ask questions to hosts or other users. A rating system allows useful questions to rise to the top, surfacing them for other users.
Messaging allows users to privately contact each other to discuss events and socalize.
Awards can be given by event hosts for exceptional helpfulness. Awards are also automatically received for completing various milestones in-app. This adds a gamification aspect to the app and helps increase retention.
