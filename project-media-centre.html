    Why wont people make my stuff
Qubit is positioning its self as an indusy leader in the word of ecommerce personalisation. As a part of this, Qubit produces a lot of reserach and useful media content for a varity of indusrty vertiacals. These include videos, webinars, events, ebooks, infographics, blog posts, case studies and more. We wanted to consolodate these resources into a one stop shop for all the latest industy insights. The challange was to create a vast feed of inforamtion while still being usable and beaufiul. People should be able to find what they are looking for based on topic and industry as well as stumble apon new and exciting information while browsing. In doing this, we hoped to create an information hub that users would return to for the latest insights into the personalisation and marketing industry.
Due to the large amount of content on the qubit site, we implamented a search and filtering system from and center of the page. This allows users to select from topics and industry vertiacls to narrow down there search and help them find what they are looking for.
An feed of useful content is categorised by industry and media type. To make sure popular and important articles dont get lost or missed, they can be highlighted for priority viewing. This allows Qubit to push the latest and more relavent content to its users.
Often Qubit will product a series of videos on special topics or for events. As these Videos are often relised over time they can easly be lost within a feed of content. We created feature blocks to allow users to easly watch through a whole set of videos one after another without leaving the page.
Mouce overlays give users more information about the content they are about to click through to. Highlighting the amount of shares adds an element of social proof, showing the user the popultiry of the content before they decide to consuming it.
Prime time for reading is ofent during the morning and evening commute. We made sure a mobile version of the webpage allowed users to easily consume content on the go. Users can still utilise the filtering system to narrow down their search for the best and more relevant content.
