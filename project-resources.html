    Why wont people make my stuff
Qubit is a big maker of things and stuff and likes to talk about some stuff and have events and blogs and stuff> the problem is making a place that people can find all this stuff. at the time it was at a place all over the place and i wanted to put it all into the same place and make a place that people wanted to come back to over time to make it the place people went for this kinda stuff. Check out some of the issue of this project.
To make sure only Qubit employees can access the comms portal we used google single sign on. Only people with a qubit email address will be able to log on and see Qubit news.
Priority news is front and centre of the design, making sure no employ misses out on the big announcements. Time zones of all the Qubit offices are displayed across the bottom of the screen to help people when booking in meetings across regions of the globe.
A Qubit newsfeed is updated by admins from every department all around the world. This means Qubti employees gain a holistic vision of company activity.
A privacy switch removes all potentially sensitive material from the screen so employees can present using their laptops without worrying about showing confidential information.
Employees can access a mobile version of the portal via a URL allowing them to keep up to date with company activity while on the road.
