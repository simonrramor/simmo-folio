  • Website
    Qubit.com
Qubit is an online personalization platform for e-commerce businesses. Myself and a team of creatives maintain and update this website with product releases, new resources and information for clients and prospects. We faced many challenges with the content of the website due to the diverse range of our target audience. The site must appeal to both marketers and various level of techies. We must also show the full range of a very complex product without over complicating and cluttering the website.

In order to create a light but valuable user experience, we created segment-specific content, including ebooks, videos, infographics, animations and more. My roll involved dictating the strategy and direction of the site as well as animation of Ui assets, creation of resources as well as website page updates.
