    Qubit.com
Qubit is an online personalization platform for e-commerce businesses. With a team of creatives, I maintain and update the website with product releases, new resources, and information for clients and prospects. Many challenges came when creating the content of the website due to the diverse range of our target audience. The site must appeal to both marketers and more technical figures too. To show the full range of a very complex product without over complicating and cluttering the website was a necessity.

To create a light but valuable user experience, segment-specific content was created, including ebooks, videos, infographics and animations. My roll involves dictating the strategy and direction of the site as well as animation of Ui assets, creation of resources and site design.
